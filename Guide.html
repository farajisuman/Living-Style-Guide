What is a living style guide?

A living style guide is a reference for a team of designers and developers to understand how an application will look and feel. It is used to convey design language, define design rationale, and explain how to use markup. A living style guide creates shared understanding among all team members and is a place for the whole team to reference how something should look and feel.

Why is a living style guide useful to developers and designers?

Living style guide helps the development and design teams to move at a faster pace as it lets a developer to quickly build a feature that inherits the already exited design of the app. As a result, developer can avoid duplicated CSS. Style guides also help designers translate existing brand guidelines to the world of the web. A living style guide acts as a personalized bootstrap that another team of developers and designers can pick up, work with, and build upon.
